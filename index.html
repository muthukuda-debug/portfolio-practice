What is journey?

Journey is a process of traveling from one place to another. It can be a physical journey, such as traveling to a new city or country, or it can be a metaphorical journey, such as personal growth or self-discovery. Journeys often involve challenges and obstacles, but they can also lead to new experiences and opportunities for learning and growth. Many people find that the journey itself is just as important as the destination, as it allows them to learn more about themselves and the world around them.
